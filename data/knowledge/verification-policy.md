# Verification Policy

## Required documents
Customers may be asked for proof of identity, proof of address, source of funds, and transaction purpose depending on transfer size and risk profile.

## High value transfers
Large transfers should trigger enhanced due diligence. The review should confirm customer identity, source of funds, destination country risk, and beneficiary details.

## Suspicious activity
Suspicious patterns include repeated failed payments, unusual destination changes, sudden high-value transfers, or risk scores above the configured threshold.
