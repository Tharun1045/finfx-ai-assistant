# Transfer FAQ

## Transfer timelines
International transfers usually complete within one to three working days. Timelines depend on currency, destination country, bank cut-off times, and compliance checks.

## Payment delays
A payment may be delayed because beneficiary details are incorrect, the receiving bank requires additional checks, the transfer missed a bank cut-off time, or the transaction is under compliance review.

## Failed transfers
Failed transfers should be reviewed for invalid account details, insufficient funds, unsupported destination routes, or rejected beneficiary bank responses.
